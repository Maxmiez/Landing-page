Types of Aliens

Grays: Small humanoids, gray or green skin tone. They are hairless, have big heads without nose or ears and black eyes. One of the most common type of alien.

Reptilians: Tall reptile humanoids, oftenly associated with conspiracy theories.

Pleiadians: Tall humanoids often with long blonde hair, pale skin and blue eyes. Also known as Nordic Aliens.

Andromedan: Tall humanoid, with light blue skin and no body hair.
